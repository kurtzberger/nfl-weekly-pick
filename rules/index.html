Scoring

The scoring for this league will follow the "Ranked Pick 'em" format. That is, each week, along with picking each team you predict to win, you'll also rank each game 1 to the number of games played that week. For example, if there are 16 games to be played for that week, you'll rank those games 1 through 16.

Upon completion of a game*, you will receive points equal to the rank that you assigned for the corresponding game if your prediction was correct; however, if your prediction was in correct, you will lose those points. Because of this, it is recommended to rank games you're more confident with a higher point value and rank games you're either unsure of or attempting to pick an upset with a lower point value. Please note: your season starting score will be 0, but there is no minimum score, meaning it is possible for your score to drop below zero.

Tiebreaker

In the event that two or more players have the same amount of points, the tiebreaker will go to the player who has the higher win percentage (i.e., superior point percentage). Win percentage is calculated by the player's number of games correctly predicted divided by the number of completed games.

$$ winPercent = {numberOfWins \over numberOfCompleteGames}$$
